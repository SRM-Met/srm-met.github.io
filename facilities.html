Research Facilities

SRM-ATMOS | Weather and Climate Extremes Research Group

Research Infrastructure

The Atmospheric Science Research Laboratory (ASRL) at SRM Institute of Science and Technology is equipped with advanced atmospheric monitoring instruments, weather observation systems and computational resources to support research in weather and climate extremes, atmospheric electricity, lightning science, satellite meteorology and numerical weather prediction.

High Performance Computing Centre (HPCC)

SRM Institute of Science and Technology maintains a state-of-the-art High Performance Computing Centre (HPCC) that supports large-scale numerical simulations, climate modelling, machine learning applications and atmospheric data processing.

  • Weather Research and Forecasting (WRF) simulations
  • Climate model analysis and CMIP6 evaluation
  • Satellite data processing and visualization
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning applications
  • Big data analytics and atmospheric modelling
  • Parallel computing and high-performance workflows

The HPCC facility plays a vital role in supporting operational weather forecasting research and climate studies undertaken by SRM-ATMOS.

Atmospheric Observation Facilities

Aethalometer

Model: AE31

Used for measurement of black carbon aerosols and atmospheric particulate matter characteristics.

Sun Photometer

Model: SL11

Used for aerosol optical depth measurements and atmospheric aerosol characterization.

Automatic Weather Station

Model: Varsha-TRG1

Provides continuous measurements of meteorological parameters including temperature, humidity, pressure, rainfall and wind.

Mini Micro Pulse LIDAR

Model: Mini MPL532

Remote sensing system for atmospheric boundary layer and aerosol profile measurements.

Research Capabilities

Weather Monitoring

Continuous monitoring of meteorological parameters using automatic weather stations and atmospheric sensors.

Aerosol Studies

Measurement of atmospheric aerosols, black carbon and particulate matter for climate and air quality studies.

Boundary Layer Research

Investigation of atmospheric boundary layer structure and aerosol vertical distribution using LIDAR observations.

Numerical Weather Prediction

High-resolution weather forecasting and climate modelling using advanced computational resources.
