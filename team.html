SRM-Met | Weather and Climate Extremes Research Group

Team Overview

SRM-Met is an Weather and Climate Extremes Research Group operating under the Atmospheric Science Research Laboratory (ASRL), Department of Physics and Nanotechnology, SRM Institute of Science and Technology.

The group consists of faculty members, doctoral researchers and collaborators working in weather and climate extremes, lightning science, atmospheric electricity, numerical weather prediction, climate modelling, satellite meteorology and artificial intelligence applications.
