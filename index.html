Welcome to SRM-ATMOS

The Atmospheric Science Research Laboratory (ASRL) is a research laboratory in the Department of Physics and Nanotechnology at SRM Institute of Science and Technology. The laboratory integrates atmospheric observations, satellite meteorology, lightning measurements, numerical weather prediction, climate modelling and artificial intelligence to investigate high-impact weather and climate events.

Our research spans weather and climate extremes, thunderstorms, lightning science, atmospheric electricity, satellite meteorology, climate variability, numerical weather prediction and physics-informed artificial intelligence.
