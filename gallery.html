CLIQ 2025 – Climate Quiz Contest

The Climate Quiz Contest (CLIQ 2025) was organized by the Atmospheric Science Research Laboratory (ASRL) in connection with World Ozone Day on 16th September 2025. The programme aimed to promote awareness of climate change, atmospheric sciences, weather hazards and environmental sustainability among students.
