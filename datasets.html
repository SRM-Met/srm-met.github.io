Overview

Research activities at SRM-ATMOS rely on a wide range of observational, reanalysis, satellite and model datasets for investigating weather and climate extremes, lightning activity, atmospheric electricity, numerical weather prediction and climate variability.

Observational Datasets

IMD Gridded Rainfall

High-resolution rainfall observations used for monsoon studies, extreme rainfall analysis and model validation.

WWLLN Lightning Data

World Wide Lightning Location Network observations for lightning climatology, thunderstorm studies and lightning hazard assessment.

Doppler Weather Radar

Radar observations used for convective storm monitoring and severe weather analysis.

Satellite Datasets

INSAT

Indian geostationary satellite observations for cloud monitoring, convection and weather analysis.

GPM IMERG

Global precipitation estimates used for rainfall monitoring and model evaluation.

TRMM

Tropical Rainfall Measuring Mission products for precipitation climatology and rainfall extremes.

MODIS

Land surface temperature, vegetation and environmental monitoring products.

Reanalysis Datasets

ERA5 Reanalysis

Atmospheric and surface variables used for weather diagnostics, climate studies and WRF simulations.

NCEP Reanalysis

Large-scale atmospheric circulation and climate variability analysis.

Climate Model Datasets

CMIP6 Simulations

Climate change projections, monsoon variability and extreme precipitation assessment.

CORDEX South Asia

Regional climate projections for impact assessment and adaptation studies.

Numerical Modelling Resources

WRF-ARW

High-resolution numerical weather prediction simulations.

WRF-ELEC

Lightning and thunderstorm electrification modelling studies.

Physics-Informed AI

Artificial intelligence frameworks for weather and climate applications.
