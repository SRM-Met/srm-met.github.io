Research Datasets

Atmospheric Science Research Laboratory

Weather and Climate Extremes Research Group

Overview

SRM-ATMOS utilizes a wide range of atmospheric, satellite, lightning, rainfall and climate datasets for research on weather and climate extremes, lightning science, atmospheric electricity, climate variability, numerical weather prediction and artificial intelligence applications.

Dataset Organization Applications

NCEP-FNL NCEP WRF initial and boundary conditions
MERRA-2 NASA Aerosol and atmospheric studies

Model Outputs Generated at SRM-ATMOS

Extreme Rainfall Studies

AI-Based Weather Applications

Development of machine learning and physics-informed AI frameworks.

Satellite-Based Products

Derived products from INSAT, GPM and other remote sensing platforms.

Data Access Policy

Most datasets listed above are publicly available through their respective agencies. Datasets generated within SRM-ATMOS may be shared for academic and collaborative research purposes subject to laboratory policies and data-sharing agreements.

Future Data Repository

SRM-ATMOS is developing a research data repository to facilitate dissemination of processed datasets, model outputs and research products generated by the laboratory.
