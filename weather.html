Weather Monitoring and Forecasting

The SRM-Met Weather Dashboard provides weather monitoring and forecasting resources relevant to atmospheric science research, teaching and outreach activities. The dashboard supports studies on weather and climate extremes, lightning activity, satellite meteorology and numerical weather prediction.

WWLLN VLF Spectrogram – SRMIST Chennai Station

Real-time Very Low Frequency (VLF) spectrogram recorded by the SRMIST Chennai station of the World Wide Lightning Location Network (WWLLN). It monitors lightning activity and provides insights into thunderstorm development and weather conditions. The image is automatically updated every 30 minutes. The vertical lines in the spectrogram indicate individual lightning-generated impulses received from regional thunderstorms.
